About Crop Yield Prediction

Utilizing machine learning models, our crop yield prediction for the Terai region leverages environmental data, including temperature,Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Humidity, Area, Season Name, Crop Name, State Name, Potassium, precipitation, and soil types.
